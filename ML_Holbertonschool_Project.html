Azerbaijan Hotel Industry Financials (2015-2024)

Financial performance of the hospitality sector in Azerbaijan, measured in thousands of AZN (min manat).

1. Revenue (Gəlirlər)

Total Revenue: Aggregate income from all sources.

Room Sales: Revenue from guest accommodations.

Public Catering (F&B): Income from restaurants and cafes.

Health Services: Income from medical/wellness treatments.

Sair Gəlirlər: Other miscellaneous income.

2. Expenses (Xərclər)

Total Expenses: Aggregate operational and administrative costs.

Fuel & Energy: Electricity, gas, and heating.

F&B Procurement: Costs for food and beverage inventory.

Wages: Employee salaries and compensation.

Water: Spending on water consumption.

Rent: Leasing payments for property/land.

Taxes: Payments to the state budget.

Maintenance: Repairs for facilities and equipment.

Digər Xərclər: All other operational expenses.

Revisiting Extrapolation with Linear Regression and Polynomial Features



Since tree-based models like GradientBoostingRegressor struggle with extrapolation, even with polynomial features, we will use a LinearRegression model. A linear model, when combined with polynomial features, can effectively capture and extrapolate non-linear trends. We will re-create polynomial features and train a LinearRegression model for future predictions.
